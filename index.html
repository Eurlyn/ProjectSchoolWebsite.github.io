About Our School

At Progressive STEAM-IB School, we offer an integrated curriculum that combines STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) with the IB (International Baccalaureate) framework. Our goal is to nurture innovative thinkers, global citizens, and lifelong learners.

Our Curriculum

  • Student-Centered Learning: Tailored to individual needs and interests.
  • Inquiry-Based Learning: Hands-on, project-based education to encourage exploration.
  • Global Perspective: Developing cultural awareness and ethical leadership.
  • STEAM Integration: Connecting disciplines to solve real-world problems.
  • Holistic Development: Mental, emotional, and physical growth for balanced citizens.
